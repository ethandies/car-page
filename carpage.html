This is a Burrito

Ingredients:

1 pound groud beef (or any meat),

1/4 cup chopped onion,

1 can (15 ounces) chili with beans,

1 1/4 cups chunky salsa,

1/4 cup chopped green chiles,

1-2 cups of cheese (your choice),
